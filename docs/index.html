Results from (epi)genomics, transcriptomics, (phospho)proteomics, metabolomics and many more experiments can be visualized onto pathway diagrams from WikiPathways and KEGG. As long as your data can be linked to genes, proteins, or metabolites, you can visualize it using PinPath.
